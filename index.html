Welcome to the pediatric dental practice of Dr. Sara Babich The first dental visit for your child is designed to create a zone of comfort, safety and fun. The goal is to have your child visit us as early as possible in their tooth development in order to create a warm, inviting anxiety free, fun visit. Acclimating your child to our friendly office and Dr. Sara’s gentle care is the key to future happy smiles. We understand that when introduced to strange surroundings, a young child can feel uncomfortable. We turn this around to be a positive reinforcement as long as the parent is on board, by assuring the child that by the end of the first visit, that no harm came to the child, and he met nice new people, played with new toys and we are all here to make new friends. We encourage parents to bring to bring the child in about 18 months old. We, of course, see, any child, at any time when there are particularly pressing issues or questions. We therefore, do encourage the non-adversarial, non-emergency visit. Following this protocol, makes all other future visits much more pleasant and anxiety free. Welcoming your child with our smiles is the formula for many future visits of happy and healthy smiles for years to come. Read More About Dr. Sara
